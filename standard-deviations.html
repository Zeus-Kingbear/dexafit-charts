What a standard deviation means
Every score on your report is a spot on this curve
Most people cluster near the middle; few sit at the edges. One standard deviation (SD) is one step from average. Your T-score and Z-score are SD scores — how many steps you are from the mean. Drag the score, or tap the curve, to see the percentile and how rare it is.
